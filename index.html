TOURISM IN PAKISTAN:




Pakistan is rich in its tourist destinations offering diverse range of choices for different types of tourists. The country home to one of the oldest civilizations
in the world, has innumerable locations of scenic beauty, world highest mountains, many religious, historic places, unique arts and crafts and a rich culture
and heritage.Pakistan is home to a number of UNESCO world heritage sites like, Archaeological Ruins at Moenjodaro, Buddhist Ruins of Takht-i-Bahi and
Neighbouring City Remains Sahr-i-Bahlol, Fort and Shalamar Gardens in Lahore, Historical Monuments at Makli, Thatta, Rohtas Fort and Taxila. Pakistan,
officially the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, is a country in South Asia. It is the world's fifth-most populous country, with a population of almost 243 million
people, and has the world's second-largest Muslim population just behind Indonesia.
